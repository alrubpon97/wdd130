Rafting Travel

Rafting Travel connects people to adventure through rivers, offering safe and thrilling experiences that celebrate teamwork, respect for nature, and exploration. Guided by the creed “flow with courage, paddle with unity,” we believe every trip is a chance for self-discovery. Our motto: ride the current, embrace the adventure.

History

Rafting Travel was founded in 2012 with a simple idea: to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with adventurers from all walks of life. What began as a small team of passionate river guides with a single raft quickly grew into a trusted company known for its commitment to safety, exploration, and unforgettable experiences.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our routes, welcomed thousands of guests, and built a reputation for turning every trip into a story worth telling. Today, Rafting Travel continues to carry forward that original spirit of adventure, guiding people through roaring rapids and serene waterways alike.
