Portugal

Portugal is a country located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula, bordered by Spain and the Atlantic Ocean. Known for its rich history of exploration, charming cities, and stunning coastlines, Portugal blends tradition with modern culture. Its capital, Lisbon, is famous for its colorful neighborhoods, fado music, and historic landmarks, while Porto is celebrated for its port wine and riverside views. With beautiful beaches in the Algarve, UNESCO World Heritage sites, and delicious cuisine, Portugal is a vibrant destination that offers both cultural depth and natural beauty.
